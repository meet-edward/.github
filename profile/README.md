## 👋 About

Edward is SEO automation software that finds what your customers search for, writes the content to match, and publishes it to your website. It handles keyword research, writing, technical fixes, and reporting, so you can put your SEO on autopilot without hiring a team or learning the work yourself.

As an AI SEO content writer, Edward studies your business and your competitors, then ships pages that rank on Google and on AI search tools like ChatGPT.

## ⚙️ What it does

- **Keyword research** that finds the high-intent terms your customers type into search, backed by industry data.
- **AI SEO content writer** that produces buyer guides, FAQs, comparison pages, and local pages in your brand voice.
- **Technical SEO** that fixes meta tags, titles, internal links, broken pages, and schema markup.
- **Competitor tracking** that watches what rivals publish and where your market is moving.
- **Reporting** that shows clicks, impressions, rankings, and how each page performs.
